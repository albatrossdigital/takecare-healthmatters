The purpose of this guide is to help Colorado criminal justice agencies, health care professionals and community organizations locate professional resources to assist justice involved individuals who qualify for Medicaid.

A person's specific status and interaction with the criminal justice system may affect Medicaid eligibility and other health care options. This online guide provides county level health-related and criminal justice-related information to help professionals in these fields build relationships and assist their clients with better access to care.

This guide also provides links to the Blue Guide Colorado, an extensive resource that helps individuals, families and community organizations locate health care providers and health care access assistance sites.
